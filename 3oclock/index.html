3 o'clock
3 o'clock is the strange puzzle game where you try to figure out who or what killed the Canderwalt family. This game will take you through a 15 minute journey in the house where it happened. You will be able to point and click through the entire story, but there's a catch! You must uncover what happened by doing the crime yourself! Will you be able to solve the mystery?
